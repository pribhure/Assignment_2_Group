Amuay Wildlife Park

Welcome to Amuay Wildlife Park, a breathtaking sanctuary nestled amidst 72 square kilometers of diverse landscapes in Venezuela.

Embark on an unforgettable journey of discovery as you explore our vibrant ecosystems, teeming with an incredible array of wildlife. From the majestic jaguar prowling the grasslands to the playful capuchin monkeys swinging through the trees, Amuay offers a unique opportunity to witness the wonders of nature up close.

Explore in the park's diverse offerings!

Discover the rich tapestry of life at Amuay through our:

  • Guided Tours: Led by knowledgeable park rangers who will share fascinating insights into the park's flora and fauna.
  • Education: Delve deeper into the park's rich history dedicated to protecting this irreplaceable ecosystem.
  • Connect: Create lasting memories with your loved ones as you hike through breathtaking landscapes, photograph awe-inspiring wildlife encounters, or simply relax and reconnect with nature.

Amuay Wildlife Park is more than just a destination; it's an experience. We invite you to explore, learn, and connect with the wonders of the natural world.

Plan your visit today and embark on an unforgettable adventure!
